About Us

We are a small community of believers who fellowship and exchange the knowledge of God. We considered as a family of God who takes care of each other.
We aim to provide true believers with the truth found in the word of God.
We fast and pray to receive the wisdom of God and transform into the person God intends us to be. We are a registered church since 2023. As we don’t have an actual location yet and our members are from different countries and locations, sermons are held online as of now.

Our pastor


Elena S. Johnsen


She was born in Thailand and raised&grew up in Norway. She is married to her husband Børge Johnsen. They have three wonderful children raised in Christian foundation. Pastor Elena was a Christian early on in life. But she truly became a believer when life hit her through unexpected illness and that is when she decided to sincerely surrender herself to the Lord Jesus Christ and follow his steps. 2022, when covid happened the way the truth and the life church was born. Process of becoming an official church was taking a whole year and 2023 the way, the truth and the life church was officially registered as a church. We have mostly members with believers coming from Thailand and living in Europe (norway&germany). Therefore the teachings are held in thai language. Other languages will follow in future.

Our Beliefs

We believe in :
  • Jesus Christ, as our Lord and Savior (acts 10:36, rom. 10:9-11, 1. john 4:14)
  • Jesus being the Way, the Truth and the Life (john 14:6)
    • He is the only Way no one can come to the Father, except through Him!
  • the Bible being the Word of God (john 1:1, hebrew 4:12, 2. timothy 3:16-17)
  • the Trinity. (Father, Son and the Holy Spirit)
  • keeping the commandment of Jesus Christ (john 14:15, matthew 28:19)
  • true repentance (matthew 4:17)
  • glorifying the father through the works he calls us to do( matthew 5:16)
  • god’s given law in the old testament (matthew 5:17)
  • choosing the narrow way (matthew 7:13-14)
  • having discernment (matthew 7 :15-16)
  • the constitution of marriage between man and woman (matthew 19:4-6)
  • the life God created ( genesis 1:27, job 33:4, psalm 100:3, psalm 119:73, psalm 139 13-16, isaiah 44:24)
