Our beliefs, mission & vision

Our beliefs

We believe that Jesus Christ is our Lord and Saviour (Acts 10:36, Rome 10:9-11, 1. John 4:14) and that Jesus Christ is the Way, the Truth and the Life. He is the Only way, no one can come to the Father except through Him.(John 14:6) Therefore we believe in the Trinity (God the Father, the Son & the Holy Spirit)
We believe that the Bible is the Word of God (John 1:1, Hebrew 4:12, 2.Timothy 3:16-17), which we need to follow unconditionally and thus need to keep the commandment of Jesus Christ (john 14:15, matthew 28:19). By following Him we glorify the Father through the works he calls us to do( matthew 5:16). We believe that God’s given law in the old testament is not eliminated (matthew 5:17). Choosing the narrow way is keeping God’s commandments (matthew 7:13-14). True repentance is essential in going the narrow road to heaven (matthew 4:17).
We believe in the constitution of marriage between man and woman (matthew 19:4-6) and in the life God created ( genesis 1:27, job 33:4, psalm 100:3, psalm 119:73, psalm 139 13-16, isaiah 44:24)

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread the Truth to the world and provide a new translation that is leaned on the original. So that believers will have discernment and a better understanding of the Word of God.
My people are destroyed for lack of knowledge: because thou hast rejected knowledge, I will also reject thee, that thou shalt be no priest to me: seeing thou hast forgotten the law of thy God, I will also forget thy children. (Hosea 4:6)

Our vision

Our Vision is to see people get saved without going astray, when facing challenges. We want to see believers mature in their faith and become one of God’s soldiers. Thou therefore endure hardness, as a good soldier of Jesus Christ. No man that warreth entangleth himself with the affairs of this life; that he may please him who hath chosen him to be a soldier. (2 Timothy 2:3-4)

Our pastor


Elena S. Johnsen

She was born in Thailand and grew up in Norway. She is married and have three wonderful children raised in Christian foundation. Pastor Elena was a Christian early on in life.
But she truly became a believer when life hits her through an unexpected illness and that is when she decided to sincerely surrender herself to the
Lord Jesus Christ and follow his steps. She then got a revelation from the Lord of founding a church and obeyed.
In 2022, when covid happened the Lord opened the way for the Way, the Truth & the Life Church. In 2023, the Way, the Truth & the Life Church was officially registered.
Pastor Elena continuously learns about the Word of God and shares it with other believers. She is a godly example of a believer, a wife and a mother.
